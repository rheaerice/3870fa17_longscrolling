Let's throw together a bowl of guacamole!

Prep Time

Ingredients

  • 3 halved, seeded, and peeled avocados
  • 1 lime juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon each of salt, cayenne, & ground cumin
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and diced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

You're All Done!

Allow to sit at room temperature for 1 hour before serving.Enjoy!
